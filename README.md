## Dignity Points – Nonprofit Website

This is a simple, modern, mobile-friendly static website for **Dignity Points**, a nonprofit organization.

### Tech stack

- **HTML5** for structure
- **CSS3** (Flexbox & Grid) for layout, responsiveness, and smooth transitions
- **Vanilla JavaScript** for navigation interactions and scroll-triggered animations

### Pages

- `index.html` – Home (hero, about intro, impacts, services overview, team preview, booking CTA, upcoming event)
- `about.html` – About Dignity Points (who we are, history, community impacts, why choose us)
- `what-we-do.html` – What We Do (high-level overview of work and focus areas)
- `projects.html` – Projects (highlight of current and past initiatives)
- `services.html` – Services (detailed list of services and focus areas)
- `all-pages.html` – All Pages (simple sitemap-style overview and quick links)
- `contact.html` – Let’s Talk / Booking (consultation booking form and contact details)

All pages share a consistent navigation bar and footer.

### Development

Open `index.html` directly in a browser or use a simple static server (for example, `npx serve .`) to view the site locally.
